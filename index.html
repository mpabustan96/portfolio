About

Design is curiosity made visible.

I'm a self-taught UI/UX designer who came up through bootcamps and obsessive independent study. No formal degree — just a genuine need to understand why some products feel effortless and others feel like a fight.

I care about the decisions that don't show up in the final file: the research that killed a bad idea early, the stakeholder tension that shaped the structure, the small copy choice that makes a hesitant person feel safe enough to reach out.
